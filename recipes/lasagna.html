Lasagna

Layers of gooey, mouth-watering scrumptiousness!

Italy's best dish!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Feta Cheese
  • Parsely

Steps

  1. Bake the ground beef
  2. Boil lasagna pasta
  3. Take beef out of oven - cook with tomato sauce in pan
  4. Apply one layer of pasta to pan - then beef layer - then add cheese - repeat until all ingredients are used
  5. Bake on high for 20 minutes
