Tuna Melt

A delicious and easy dinner sandwich.

Warm up the night with a toasty tuna melt on buttery toast!

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Tuna
  • Tomato
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the bread
  2. Bake tuna
  3. Let tuna sit - apply cheese
  4. Toast bread
  5. Put tuna on bread - apply tomato
